Hands-on Challenge: Business Process Mapping (Mondocorp)
This challenge demonstrated the core Salesforce business flow by tracking a new deal from initial contact to a qualified opportunity.
1. The Business Scenario
   The goal was to establish a relationship with a new customer, Mondocorp, and track a potential sale for a replacement gas generator.
2. Record Creation Steps
   I performed the following data entries in the Sales App:
   Account (The Company): Created the Mondocorp account, categorized under the Energy industry with 10,000 employees.
   Contact (The Person): Created a contact for Shawn Corbin, linking him directly to the Mondocorp account to maintain a single source of truth.
   Opportunity (The Deal): Logged the Replacement gas generator deal. I set the stage to Value Proposition and assigned a close date to track the revenue pipeline.
3. Key Concepts Applied
   Through this challenge, I practiced the standard Salesforce object relationship model:
   Data Integrity: Corrected initial data entry errors (Employee count) to ensure the organization's records remained accurate for reporting.
   Relational Mapping: Linked the Opportunity to both the Account and the Primary Contact, ensuring a 360-degree view of the customer.
   Sales Pipeline Management: Identified how a "Lead" transitions into a qualified "Opportunity".
